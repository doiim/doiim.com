Digvisions

Search for stuff to buy

Digvisions is a price comparison application that can recognize photos taken by your WebOS device. If you want to search for something but don't know what it is, just take a picture of the product, this app will recognize it and search for you.

Features

  • Find products for a better price;
  • History to remember your latest searches;
  • Searches are based on your location.
